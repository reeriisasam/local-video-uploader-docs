Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Local Video Uploader application ("the App") handles user information.

Information Collection

The App does not collect or store user passwords or sensitive personal information.

OAuth Authentication

OAuth access tokens provided by TikTok or YouTube are used solely for the purpose of uploading videos on behalf of the user. Tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.

Data Usage

The App is used for personal and educational purposes only. Uploaded videos are processed locally and are not analyzed or shared externally.

Third-Party Services

The App interacts only with official APIs provided by TikTok and YouTube. No user data is sold or transferred to external services.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository.
